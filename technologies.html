KelForce™ High-Toughness, High-Strength Resin-Coupled Cement System

Technology Overview

KelForce™ is an advanced resin-coupled cement system developed to address well integrity challenges. It combines a specially formulated cement base with thermosetting resin, providing superior zonal isolation, long-term sealing performance, and enhanced pressure-bearing capacity.

Key Features

  • Compressive Strength > 50 MPa
  • Bonding Strength > 3.5 MPa
  • Flexible and Crack Resistant
  • Volumetric Stability (1.9% linear expansion)
  • Thermal and Chemical Resistance

System Composition

KelForce™ is a dual-component system, combining base cement slurry and thermosetting resin with functional additives. This creates a resilient polymer network, improving mechanical performance and durability under stress and thermal cycles.

Applications

Typical applications include casing leak remediation, interzonal flow isolation, water shut-off, channeling repair, and HPHT isolation.

Performance Comparison

KelForce™ outperforms standard cement in key metrics such as compressive strength, bonding strength, and pressure-bearing capability. The comparison shows over 2× the compressive strength and more than 3× the bonding strength of conventional cement.
